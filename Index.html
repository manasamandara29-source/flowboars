About the Innovation

Our team in the Innovation Lab identified that farmers lose income not due to lack of hard work, but due to decision delay. Rythu Setu simplifies the flow of information by moving from text-heavy apps to simple voice interactions.

The Interaction Flow

1. Voice Query

Farmer sends a WhatsApp voice note about a crop observation.

2. Guidance

System analyzes urgency and provides timing/risk warnings immediately.

3. Expert Link

Critical issues are flagged and escalated to a human expert.

Project Scope (MVP)

What We Address

  • Decision confusion in local languages
  • Timing guidance for field activities
  • Trusted, non-commercial voice support

Out of Scope (For Now)

  • Specific chemical/brand dosages
  • End-to-end automation
  • E-commerce or sales
